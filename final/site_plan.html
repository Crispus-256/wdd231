Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide a centralized online platform for a mentorship program aimed at helping youth avoid negative influences (e.g., drugs) and focus on personal growth, education, and spiritual development. The site will offer resources, tools, and opportunities for community involvement, while emphasizing teamwork and collaboration between mentors, donors, volunteers, and participants.

Scenarios

1. What resources are available to help me stay away from negative influences like drugs?

• This question addresses the needs of youth seeking guidance and tools to overcome challenges and remain focused on positive goals.

2. How can I become a mentor or volunteer in this program?

• This scenario targets adults or community members who want to contribute their time and skills to support the program's mission.
